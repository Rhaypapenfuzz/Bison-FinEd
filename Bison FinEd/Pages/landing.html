Who are we targeting

Our target demographic covers a wide range. We have implemented a game into our webapp in order to reach young children in the ages of 5-15. We developed a video service provider to reach young adults. And we estimated that adults would be interested in educational courses.

Our Future

Our goals for this application include: 1) We desire to implement a donation portal to support financial education in Africa. African countries are currently suffering from mismanagement of resources and we believe proper education of the youth can reverse this trend. 2) We desire to diversify the games section with more engaging games and genres in order to reach a larger audience. We believe that indoctrinating financial literacy into youths at a young age will set them up for life and our platform is a great way to achieve that.
